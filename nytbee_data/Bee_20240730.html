Welcome to the new page!

I've been working on these updates for a while and am excited to roll them out. Please send along any feedback you have!

If you value this page, consider donating. I appreciate everyone who has contributed in the past. It's been three years since the first fundraising campaign. Your help has allowed me to pay expenses such as hosting fees and to keep the page ad-free.

If you'd like to contribute but do not have PayPal, you can contact me here

The biggest changes to the page are the most-requested items from the survey last year. I've also added additional analysis and some fun facts.

  • Definitions: I suspect the most anticipated feature will be word definitions (click the arrow next to the word to open the definition, click outside the box to close). This was the hardest feature to develop, but eventually I found a good dictionary API. It doesn't have EVERY word. So you will still occasionally have to google them.
  • Date picker: Another big upgrade is a date picker to easily switch between the pages. This will be on all new pages. Hit the back button if the page you jump to doesn't have it enabled.
  • Extra Hints: A chart for when you need extra hints. It shows number of words by letter and which letters pangrams start with (in red).
  • Two new charts that show word length in the puzzle. I was amazed that over 10,000 unique words have appeared in the Bee!
  • A chart with the average word length in the puzzle and a comparison to all puzzles. I was surprised it was so short! Lots of 4- and 5-letter words out there.
  • Back-end and technical upgrades for increased stability.

A small number of people wanted to keep the old page. I will make it available here each day

Thank you for visiting my page! I'm grateful for our fun little community and for the folks I've met while running this site.
